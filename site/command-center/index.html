Runtime configuration

Controls how the agent executes in production. Dry-run mode is strongly recommended for pilot customers.
🧪 Dry-run mode
When ON, the agent detects and alerts but never executes gateway actions (mark DNW, approve timesheet, etc.). Ideal for pilot customers during first 30 days.
📱 SMS outreach enabled
Send SMS reminders to candidates via Twilio (A2P 10DLC). Requires Twilio credentials in the Integrations tab.
✉️ Email outreach enabled
Send email reminders via Postmark / SES when SMS is unavailable or opted out.
🚨 Auto-escalate to HITL on critical placements
Skip outreach and go straight to HITL queue for placements flagged as "critical" in Bullhorn.
🔍 PII redaction in logs
Redact candidate names, emails, phone numbers, and government IDs from structured logs and LangSmith traces. Strongly recommended.
📊 LangSmith tracing
Emit agent execution traces to LangSmith for observability and debugging.
🌙 Quiet hours
Suppress SMS outreach between 9 PM and 8 AM in the candidate's timezone. Email reminders still allowed.
