How It Works

From Raw Data to Searchable Intelligence

Your data goes through a five-step pipeline that transforms raw information into searchable, context-aware intelligence that any agent or employee can query.

  • Ingest: Data flows in from APIs — emails, transcripts, CRM records, documents, VMS exports
  • Chunk: Content is broken into semantic chunks optimized for retrieval
  • Embed: Each chunk becomes a high-dimensional vector for semantic understanding
  • Store: Vectors are indexed for fast similarity search across millions of records
  • Search: Hybrid search combines vector similarity, keyword matching, and AI re-ranking for precision
