The Problem

Your Middle Office Is Bleeding Revenue

Six operational gaps that cost mid-market staffing firms hundreds of thousands per year — and your existing BI stack can't close them.

⚠️

VMS ↔ Bullhorn drift

Weekly VMS feeds rarely line up cleanly with your Bullhorn placements. Name variants, rate tier drift, missing OT breakdowns — $2–5% of revenue lost in the reconciliation gap between the two systems.

⏰

Timesheet anomalies nobody chases

Missing timesheets, consecutive misses, hours variance, and silent rate drift compound across hundreds of placements every pay period — and nobody has 8–12 hours a week to run the query.

📄

Invoice mismatches block cash

Rate variances, missing OT lines, and PO discrepancies hold invoices in limbo for days. Delayed invoices mean delayed payments — DSO slips quietly by a week or two.

🛡️

Compliance gaps hide in spreadsheets

Credentials, state minimum-wage rules, background check expirations — tracked in side-sheets and rediscovered at audit. One missed renewal can shut down an engagement.

💰

Collections run on memory

Your AR team knows which clients always pay on day 47 — but that intuition isn't in any system. No predictive visibility, no structured outreach cadence, no audit trail.

🤖

You already have dashboards. You need an operator.

Power BI, Tableau, and Bullhorn reports tell you the pipeline is leaking. None of them do anything about it. What you actually need is a fleet of agents that act on what the dashboard shows — with a human in the loop on every decision.
