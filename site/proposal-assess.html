The Challenge

Staffing companies running Bullhorn One face a universal problem: operational visibility requires manual effort. Pay/bill teams spend hours each day pulling reports, cross-referencing data, and chasing missing information across disconnected systems. The result:

  • Missing timesheets become unbilled revenue (industry average: 2–5% revenue leakage)
  • Rate mismatches and compliance gaps go undetected until audit
  • Manual report-building consumes 8–12 hours per week per team member
  • No single view of operational health across placements, timesheets, payroll, billing, and invoices

The Solution: StaffingAgent Command Center

The Command Center is an operational intelligence dashboard that embeds directly in Bullhorn One. It replaces manual report-building with real-time visibility, risk detection, and one-click mass actions — all from a single interface your team already works in.

What's Included
  • 5 Entity Panels — Placement, Time & Expense, Payroll, Billing, Invoices with drill-through to Bullhorn lists
  • Risk Panel — 7 risk categories: timesheets, placement alignment, wage compliance, rate flags, hours flags, amount flags, markup analysis
  • TimeOps Dashboard — Missing timesheet management with mass reminders, exclusions, and audit trail
  • RiskOps Dashboard — Full risk alert management with resolution tracking and mass actions
  • Admin Settings — Configurable risk tolerances, placement statuses, and role-based access controls
  • Configurable Filters — Map any Bullhorn custom field as a dashboard filter
  • Implementation included — 2–3 business day deployment, 30-minute admin configuration, training session
